## ğŸ“ Overview
**CampusDine** is a mobile application concept developed to address the logistical frustrations students and staff face when accessing food on campus.

Developed as a comprehensive coursework project for the **User-Centred Systems Design** module at City, St George's, this case study demonstrates my ability to execute a full product design lifecycleâ€”from initial discovery and qualitative research to high-fidelity prototyping and usability validation.

**The Challenge:** User research revealed that students were skipping meals or arriving late to lectures due to unpredictable queues, unclear dietary labelling, and a lack of information regarding facility locations.

---

## ğŸ” 01-User-Research
To ensure the solution was grounded in real user needs, I conducted semi-structured interviews with **5 participants** (3 Students, 2 Staff). Transcripts were analysed using Thematic Analysis to identify core pain points.

**Key Insights:**
* **Queue Anxiety:** Long queues frequently caused students to be late for lectures. One participant noted, *"There was a long queue and then I was late for my lecture"*.
* **Dietary Uncertainty (Halal):** A critical finding was the lack of visible Halal certification. Users often waited in line just to ask staff if food was suitable, causing delays and stress.
* **Hidden Facilities:** Users were unaware of essential free facilities. One participant admitted, *"I didn't even know that we have microwaves in the uni"*.
* **Seating Scarcity:** Overcrowding in the main canteen often forced users to eat in corridors due to a lack of space.

## ğŸ“± 05-Wireframes
I designed 6 key screens and a user flow diagram to visualise the solution.

**Key Screens Designed:**
* **Home Screen:** Features "Quick Filters" (Hot/Cold/Halal) and live "Busyness" indicators.
* **Menu Interface:** Includes clear calorie and dietary badges (Halal/Veg) to reduce decision time.
* **Smart Map:** A dedicated feature allowing users to toggle **Seating** (Live capacity), **Microwaves**, and **Water Fountains** with status updates.
* **Basket:** Includes specific time-slot selection for pickup to align with lecture breaks.
