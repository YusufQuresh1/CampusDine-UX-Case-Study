## ðŸ“‹ 3. Task Scenarios
Participants will be asked to complete three realistic tasks using the prototype:

### Task A: Discovery & Filtering
*"You have a short break and want to find a specific meal. Locate a **Halal-friendly** food outlet and browse the available menu choices."*
* **Goal:** Test the visibility and logic of the "Quick Filters" and dietary badges.

### Task B: Pre-Ordering
*"You are currently in a lecture and want to grab lunch immediately after. Select a meal, customise it (e.g., add sides), and **pre-order it for pickup**."*
* **Goal:** Assess the user experience of the ordering flow, basket, and payment sections.

### Task C: Facility Navigation
*"You have collected your food but the canteen is full. Use the app to find the nearest **available seating area**."*
* **Goal:** Evaluate the effectiveness of the interactive Map layer and live status indicators.

We will capture both qualitative and quantitative data:

* **Single Ease Question (SEQ):** Administered after *each task* (Rated 1-7) to measure immediate task difficulty.
